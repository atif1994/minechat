Q: What is MineChat?
A: MineChat is an AI-powered chatbot application that helps businesses automate customer support and communication.

Q: How does MineChat work?
A: MineChat uses artificial intelligence to understand customer queries and provide relevant responses based on your business information, products, and FAQs.

Q: What file formats are supported for FAQ upload?
A: MineChat supports .txt and .csv file formats for uploading FAQ data.

Q: Can I customize the AI responses?
A: Yes, you can customize AI responses by providing your business information, products, services, and frequently asked questions.

Q: Is MineChat secure?
A: Yes, MineChat uses Firebase authentication and secure cloud storage to protect your data and customer information.
